## Method

1. Cook the rice and let it cool slightly.
2. Marinate the cubed salmon in soy sauce and sesame oil for 10 minutes.
3. Assemble bowls: rice, salmon, cucumber, edamame, spring onions.

## Notes

Best eaten within 2 days. Keep the salmon marinating separately if prepping ahead.
