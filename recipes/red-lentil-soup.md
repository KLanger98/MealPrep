## Method

1. Heat the olive oil and soften the onion and carrot, about 8 minutes.
2. Add garlic and cumin, cook 1 minute.
3. Add lentils, tomatoes and stock. Simmer 25 minutes until the lentils collapse.
4. Blend half the soup for texture, or all of it for smooth. Season.

## Notes

Freezes well. Thickens in the fridge — loosen with water when reheating.
