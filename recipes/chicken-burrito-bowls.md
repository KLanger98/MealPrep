## Method

1. Toss the chicken with olive oil, smoked paprika and salt. Pan-fry over medium-high heat until cooked through, about 12 minutes. Rest, then slice.
2. Meanwhile cook the rice.
3. Warm the beans and corn.
4. Portion rice, chicken, beans and corn into containers. Squeeze lime over each just before eating.

## Notes

Keeps 4 days refrigerated. Add avocado fresh on the day.
