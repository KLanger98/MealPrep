## Method

1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot. Brown the beef mince in batches, then set aside.
2. Soften the onion, add garlic, cumin and chilli powder, cook 1 minute.
3. Return the beef with tomatoes and half a can of water. Simmer 45 minutes.
4. Stir through the kidney beans for the last 10 minutes. Season with salt.

## Notes

Freezes brilliantly for up to 3 months. Serve over rice or with corn chips.
