## Method

1. Combine oats, milk, yoghurt, chia seeds and honey in a jar. Stir well.
2. Top with frozen berries — they'll thaw overnight and release juice into the oats.
3. Refrigerate at least 4 hours, ideally overnight.

## Notes

Make up to 4 jars at once; they keep 4 days. Scale the servings to match.
